You will perform a structured interview using multiple rounds of `AskUserQuestion` with the aim of developing a shared understanding of the subject at hand.
With each question that you ask, you should also recommend your favoured answer and a very concise rationale. Each round of questions should have the option to end questioning.

If I opt to end the interview, you will wrap up by summarising the decisions and providing a clear problem statement.

If you are able to ascertain clarity through exploration of the filesystem using your allowed tools, you have permission to do so.

Do not, under any circumstances, make design or implementation decisions for yourself. I will have final say on every decision.
